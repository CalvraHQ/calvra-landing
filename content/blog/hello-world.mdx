## Why we're building this

Every great tool starts with a frustration. Ours was simple: the way teams work today involves too much overhead, too many context switches, and too little time doing the actual work.

We started Calvra to change that.

## What we believe

We believe the best software is invisible. It fits your workflow rather than forcing you to adapt to it. It makes the right thing to do the easy thing to do.
